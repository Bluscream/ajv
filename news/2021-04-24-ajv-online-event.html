Ajv online event - May 20, 10am PT / 6pm UK

April 24, 2021

We will talk about:

  • new features of Ajv version 8.
  • the improvements sponsored by Mozilla's MOSS grant.
  • how Ajv is used in JavaScript applications.

Speakers:

  • Evgeny Poberezkin (opens new window), the creator of Ajv.
  • Mehan Jayasuriya (opens new window), Program Officer at Mozilla Foundation, leading the MOSS (opens new window) and other programs investing in the open source and community ecosystems.
  • Matteo Collina (opens new window), Technical Director at NearForm and Node.js Technical Steering Committee member, creator of Fastify web framework.
  • Kin Lane (opens new window), Chief Evangelist at Postman. Studying the tech, business & politics of APIs since 2010. Presidential Innovation Fellow during the Obama administration.
  • Ulysse Carion (opens new window), the creator of JSON Type Definition specification.

Gajus Kuizinas (opens new window) will host the event.
